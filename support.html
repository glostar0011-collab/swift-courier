FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I track a high-value asset?
Simply enter your Consignment ID into the main terminal. High-value assets are monitored via our Security Matrix, providing minute-by-minute geospatial telemetry.
What is the "Security Matrix"?
The Security Matrix is a multi-layered protocol involving AES-256 data encryption and biometric verification at every physical hand-off point in the supply chain.
How do I change my delivery address?
Address changes can be requested up until the cargo reaches the "Local Distribution" stage. Please contact your regional dispatch office immediately.
Do you offer cold-chain logistics?
Yes. Our Healthcare Division provides validated temperature-controlled environments for pharmaceutical and biological assets worldwide.
